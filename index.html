Democracy Dies in Darkness

Shedding Light on Diversity in Tech

If you walk into a tech department or industry, chances are you'll see a working environment that mainly consists of males, either White or Asian. Though the biggest tech companies with this certain demographic still flourish in their success, the gap in gender and racial diversity still takes a toll . There are so many benefits to having people of different color and minds in tech.

By: Linh Ly
November 8th, 2018
